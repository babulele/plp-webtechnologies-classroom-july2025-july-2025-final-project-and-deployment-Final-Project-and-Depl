Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while complex web applications can take 2-6 months. I provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes! I offer maintenance and support packages to keep your website updated, secure, and running smoothly. This includes regular updates, security patches, and performance monitoring.

What technologies do you work with?

I specialize in modern web technologies including React, Vue.js, Node.js, MongoDB, PostgreSQL, and various cloud platforms. I choose the best technology stack for each project.

Can you work with my existing team?

Absolutely! I'm experienced in collaborating with in-house teams, designers, and other developers. I can integrate seamlessly with your existing workflow and processes.

Do you offer training and documentation?

Yes, I provide comprehensive documentation and training sessions to help your team understand and maintain the solutions I build. This ensures long-term success for your project.

What's included in your pricing?

My pricing includes design, development, testing, deployment, and initial support. I provide detailed breakdowns so you know exactly what you're getting for your investment.
